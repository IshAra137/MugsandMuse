About the Workshop

Join us for an exciting and creative workshop where you can enjoy two delightful activities: Mug Painting and Coffee Making. This workshop is perfect for coffee enthusiasts and art lovers alike. Learn the secrets of brewing a delicious cup of coffee and unleash your creativity with custom mug designs to take home!

Workshop Schedule

  • 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM: Mug Painting Session
  • 11:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Coffee Break with Snacks
  • 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM: Coffee Making Masterclass
  • 1:30 PM - 2:00 PM: Showcase & Wrap-up

Highlights

  • Create your own custom hand-painted mug to take home.
  • Learn the art of brewing coffee from professional baristas.
  • Enjoy a coffee tasting session with snacks provided.
  • Connect with fellow coffee lovers and creatives in a cozy environment.
